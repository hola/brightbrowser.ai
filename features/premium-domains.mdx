### Activate Premium Domains

When creating your BrightBrowser.ai zone, enable the **Premium Domains** switch.

### Pricing

Once activated, the premium pricing will be displayed in the **Estimated Cost** section.

<Note>
  Even after activation, only requests to premium domains will incur the higher rate, while all other requests will remain at the standard pricing tier.
</Note>
